About NAAD

Music! Its feet stead fasted in the deepest of the seas and its head held high into the sky! Indeed, it’s something so pure, so serene! The melody of the primordial sound that leaves us enchanted, the sound of the cosmos and of human consciousness, a sound that transcends space and time, a sound that has no beginning or end, a sound that isn’t simply music, a sound that’s magic- NAAD!

Gear up folks, as Dhwani- Music Club, BIT Mesra returns with its annual inter-college music competition - NAAD! The wait is finally over and the excitement is going to take you on a roll! Get ready to witness music in its purest form through a series of electrifying events and competitions ranging from Hard-core metal rock to spellbinding tunes of Indian Classical Music! Scintillating nights and exhilarating events await!

So prepare yourselves to hit the right notes, knock up your instruments to strike the right chords and get ready to flow in a mesmerizing musical journey with us!

मंद्र MAYHEM

(Battle of Bands)

Scribbled notes roar, through rusted metal guitars. Here we create symphonies from chaos, here we make music from mayhem!


RULES:-
1. Participants should bring their own instruments. However, a basic drum kit will be provided.
2. The band needs to consist of college students. Semi-professional bands are also allowed.
3. The team size is 3-8 members.
4. Multiple teams from the same college are permitted.
5. Only one member from each team has to register for the event after the individual registration of all members is complete.
6. Lyrical content of the performances should be strictly in English/Hindi or both and any obscenity in lyrical content will lead to immediate disqualification.
7. Time limit is 15 minutes (Stage in to Stage out) and it should be strictly followed. Participants will be penalized for not doing so.
8. The time limit will be extended in case of any technical inconveniences from the organizing side.
9. The decision of the judges shall be final and binding.

Raageshree

(Classical Singing Competition)

Witness the elating spectacle of Raagas and lose yourselves in the prosperous heritage of Indian classical music with Raageshree- the Classical Solo Singing competition!

RULES:-
1. Only pure classical compositions are allowed.
2. Participants must perform solo or with a maximum of 2 accompanists can bring up to 2 accompanists for the competition but the judgement will be based only on the vocal performance.
3. No accompanying instruments are allowed except a harmonium, tanpura and tabla. Participants may bring their own instruments. A harmonium, electronic tanpura, electronic tabla and a tabla set will be provided.
4. Time limit is 10 minutes (Stage in to Stage out) and it should be strictly followed. Participants will be heavily penalized by the judges for not doing so.
5. The time limit will be extended in case of any technical inconveniences from the organizing side.
6. The decision of the judges shall be final and binding.

Contact: Arunjeet (9006812852), Sudakshina (8794547100)

Dhun

(Instrumental Competition)

The mellifluous notations of the keyboard, the divine strings of the sitar or the rocking rhythm of the guitar- immerse yourselves in the aura of symphony as NAAD presents to you the instrumental competition- Dhun!

RULES:-
1. Pure instrumental performance pertaining to any genre is allowed.
2. Participants can participate solo or can bring up to 2 accompanists for the competition but the judgement will be based only on the main instrumentalist.
3. All kinds of instruments are allowed. Participants have to bring their own instruments. Only a standard drum-kit will be provided.
4. Participants cannot use a backing track.
5. The time limit is 8 minutes (stage in- stage out). Negative marking will be done for participants who exceed the time limit.
6. The time limit will be extended in case of any technical inconveniences from the organizing side.
7. The decisions taken by the judges will be final and binding.

Contact: Devyani (6393568636), Rajarshi (7209545708)

Alankaar

(Eastern Music Competition)

With the peppy bollywood music, the soulful hindi songs or gazals, thumri, witness the splendour of symphonies in Hindi music with Alankaar- the Eastern Singing competition!

RULES:-
1. Only decent Bollywood songs and songs of Hindi language are allowed.
2. Participants can either perform solo or can bring up to 2 accompanists for the competition but the judgement will be based only on vocal performance.
3. Participants can bring their own track.
4. Time limit is 8 minutes (Stage in to Stage out) and it should be strictly followed. Participants will be heavily penalized by the judges for not doing so. Negative marking will be done.
5. The time limit will be extended in case of any technical inconveniences from the organizing side.
6. Obscenity (at the discretion of judges) of any kind in the lyrical content of the song is not allowed and will lead to immediate disqualification.
7. The decision of the judges shall be final and binding.

MAATI

(Regional Music Competition)

Gear up to flow in with the perfect blend of ritualistic folk and cultural expressions of music as NAAD brings to you the folk regional singing competition- Maati!


RULES:-
1. Only regional/folk songs are allowed.
2. The time limit is 8 minutes. Negative marking will be done on exceeding the time limit.
3. The time limit will be extended in case of any technical inconveniences from the organizing side.
4. Both solo and group performances are allowed.
5. There can be 2-7 members in a group (including instrumentalists).
6. There should not be any kind of obscenity in the lyrics.
7. The decision of the judges shall be final and binding.
